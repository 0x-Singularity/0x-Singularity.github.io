# About Me

Hello! I'm Garrett McGuire, a software engineer with a passion for malware analysis, reverse engineering, and cyber security. Welcome to my personal blog, where I share my journey, insights, and projects.

## My Journey

I started my career in cyber threat intelligence, where I developed a keen interest in understanding the intricacies of malware and the methods used by threat actors. After a year and a half in that role, I transitioned to software engineering to deepen my technical expertise and broaden my skill set.

## What I Do

- **Malware Development and Analysis:** In my free time, I enjoy developing simple malware and analyzing its behavior to better understand how it operates. This includes setting up isolated environments, using tools like Ghidra and Cutter, and writing detailed analysis reports.

- **Software Engineering:** Currently, I am working on various Java and Go projects, with a focus on improving workflows, documentation, and version control within my team. I am also exploring advanced Java concepts and AWS services to enhance my technical skills.

- **Blogging and Knowledge Sharing:** Through this blog, I aim to share my experiences, tutorials, and insights on topics related to malware analysis, software engineering, and cybersecurity.

## Interests and Hobbies

When I'm not immersed in code or dissecting malware, you can find me reading cyberpunk fiction, volunteering at animal shelters, or working on my Jeep Wrangler Sahara. I am also an avid learner, always seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow.
